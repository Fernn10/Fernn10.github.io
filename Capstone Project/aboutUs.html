About Us

About Timpanogos Indoor

The Let's Play Sports team is a group of inclusive people dedicated to creating joy and igniting meaningful relationships through our common passion for the game. We have been in business since 1987. We currently own and operate indoor soccer facilities in Colorado, Texas, Utah, Idaho, Kansas, Oklahoma, California.
